Addiction Services

At Acme, we can help exchange your addictions for healthy hobbies. We have certified addiction counselors on staff ready to meet your needs.

Drug & Alcohol

Can you imagine living a healthy, fulfilling and drug-free life? Your self esteem restored, your relationships improved, and your career benefited? Just imagine how it will feel to have control over your own life again. Rehabilitation is built on rediscovering yourself; where you can begin to understand why you have a problem and avoid repeating those patterns as you journey toward your new clean life.

Gambling

Addictive behavior often causes disruptions in any major area of life: psychological, physical, social or vocational. “Problem Gambling” is an issue that includes, but is not limited to, behavioral addictions characterized by increasing pre-occupation with gambling, a need to bet more money more frequently, restlessness or irritability when attempting to stop, “chasing” losses, and failure to recognize negative consequences.

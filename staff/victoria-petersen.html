My Story

I started out as a client...

My story with Acme Counseling began back in 2018 when I decided I was sick and tired of being sick and tired. I started services here so that I could heal from my past trauma, and learn skills and tools necessary to succeed in life. A few weeks later, I put down the alcohol and picked myself up from the unmanageable life I created.

The November of 2021 marked a checkpoint on my road to recovery when I was given the opportunity to join the Acme Counseling team as their office manager. In the short time I've been here, my job has grown to include many different roles, including billing. It is my goal here at Acme to continue my growth and understanding of substance abuse, and to pass that knowledge on to others who needed the same help I did.

...Now I use my experience to help others.

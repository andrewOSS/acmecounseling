My Story

I didn't always want to work here...

Back in September of 2014 is when I joined Acme. I knew I was never going to be a counselor - I'm not enough of a people-person - but I knew I could help in other ways. Acme had just lost the person that was running the front of the office, and they needed a qualified person to fill those shoes.

It was rough at first, trying to learn all the rules, regulations, and such - but previous experiences of mine made it easier to transition than I was expecting. According to Bobby, I "brought a wealth of office knowledge to Acme," and, "my timing could not have been better." I consider those statements about me an achievement of mine, and I'm proud to be part of the Acme team.

...But now I don't want to leave.
